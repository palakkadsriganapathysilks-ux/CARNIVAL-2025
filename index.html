About Us

At Sri Ganapathy silks, we firmly believe that there is a better way to cater the best collections of outfits to all age groups. Our founder Thiru T.M. Ganapathy Mudaliar, was obsessively passionate about creating inspiration and innovation on providing quality service to the customers. Started as a small-time shop in 1944, he carried the dream journey to open a textile showroom in 1959 in sankarankovil. To make big leaps, we in Sri Ganapathy Silks carried our founder legacy by creating a milestone, we entered Manchester of South India with one of the largest textile showrooms in Coimbatore in 2005. It was our founder's vision that we have expanded our presence with showrooms across Tamilnadu in nearly seven decades of successful journey. We at Sri Ganapathy silks always ensure the best quality service by our experienced team with our exhaustive wide range of collections at affordable prices.
